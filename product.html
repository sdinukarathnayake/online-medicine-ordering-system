About this Medicine

Paracetamol 500mg tablets are used for the relief of mild to moderate pain such as headache, toothache, muscular pain, and period pains. They are also effective in reducing fever.

Paracetamol is a well-tolerated medicine and is suitable for most people, including children, pregnant women, and elderly patients. However, it is important not to exceed the recommended dose.

Possible Side Effects

When taken at the recommended dosage, paracetamol is unlikely to cause side effects. However, some people may experience:

  • Allergic reactions such as skin rash
  • Blood disorders
  • Liver damage (if taken in excessive doses)

If you experience any side effects, stop taking the medicine and consult your doctor.

Precautions

Do not exceed the stated dose. Excessive use of paracetamol can cause liver damage. Consult your doctor if symptoms persist.
